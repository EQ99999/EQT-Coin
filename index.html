How it Works

The core idea behind EQT is to create a donation leverage effect. By linking the visibility and trading volume of the memecoin to real humanitarian aid, each participant amplifies the impact of their involvement.

1. Token Launch: 100% supply created. 90% available to public. 10% reserved for dev & ops.
2. Revenue: Sales, donations, and trading generate funds.
3. Buybacks: Funds are used to buy EQT from the market to increase value.
4. Donation: 90% of profits are sent to trusted humanitarian organizations.
5. Impact Loop: More visibility = more volume = more donations = real impact.
